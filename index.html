Our MIssion.

We have founded the United Nations for 3 core reasons.

01

Peace

Our first objective is to maintain peace, and to be a mediator on the brink of conflict.
02

Connection

Our second objective is to connect the community and leaders of Santoria together in a safe and open place.
03

Prosperity

Our third objective is to create projects to make the server a safer, better, prettier and more efficient place.

Maintaining Peace on Santoria

Meetings...?

What the hell are UN Meetings?

Every week, the United Nations meets up for a regular meeting to discuss certain events and other situations on the server. The meetings are split into 3 parts:

  • Global / Server-wide Matters (Large Conflicts, etc.)
  • UN Matters (Projects & Updates)
  • Local & Internal Matters (Small Conflicts, Civil Wars, etc.)
Join our discord to partake in meetings!
